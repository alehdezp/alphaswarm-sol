## Why This Plan Exists

The multi-agent debate is the core differentiator of AlphaSwarm.sol:
- Static analyzers produce findings directly
- AlphaSwarm.sol debates findings through attacker/defender/verifier

**If agents don't actually debate, the system is just a fancy wrapper around Slither.**

This plan proves the debate is real:
- Agents actually spawn (not just described in skills)
- Each agent produces reasoned output with evidence
- Verifier synthesizes both sides (not just picks one)
- Graph evidence flows through the entire chain
