## Executive Summary (Reality Check)

The phase is moving in the right direction because testing now has a canonical home and rules, but it is not yet usable in production because the core orchestration behavior is unproven and untested. The most critical blockers are:

- Installation and first-run have never been validated in real claude-code-agent-teams sessions.
- The audit entrypoint still behaves like a report generator, not a task-based orchestrator.
- TaskCreate and TaskUpdate markers are missing in transcripts.
- Progress guidance and resume semantics are not reliable.
- Tier C gating, economic context, and settings enforcement are missing or undefined.
- No workflow has a live claude-code-agent-teams transcript and evidence pack to prove it works.

## Definition Gate (Must Be Met Before Any Plan)

- EI and CTL definitions are documented with expected outputs.
- Tier C gating thresholds and required label coverage are documented.
- Workflow contracts exist in docs and define inputs, outputs, and success criteria.
- Orchestration markers are defined and testable.
- Alignment ledger template and workflow ref validation script are defined.
