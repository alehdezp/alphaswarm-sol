## Reasoning
Command naming drift creates confusion for users and breaks validation rules. A single naming standard is required before GA. The value is in consistent, predictable skill invocation.

## Purpose

## Why
Naming alignment is blocking because inconsistent slash command formats leak into workflows, documentation, and testing gates.
