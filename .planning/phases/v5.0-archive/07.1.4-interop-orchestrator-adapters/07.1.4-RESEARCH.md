# Phase 7.1.4 Research

## Summary
Production-grade multi-agent orchestration in 2025-2026 is converging around a handful of frameworks and platform primitives: Agents SDK (handoffs + guardrails + tracing), LangGraph (state graphs + persistence), AutoGen (team/chat orchestration), and CrewAI (roles + flows). Git-backed orchestration systems (Beads, Gas Town) add durable, dependency-aware tasking.

## Applied Takeaways
- Adapter design should align handoff semantics and trace propagation.
- Evidence contracts must survive framework boundaries.
- Persistence primitives (checkpointers) should map to bead state replays.
