## Decisions Made

1. **Unique socket per run**: Each claude-code-agent-teamsHarness gets `/tmp/vrs-{run_id}.sock` to prevent collisions in parallel test execution
2. **Deterministic pane roles**: Controller=index 0 (created with session), Subject/Monitor=indices 1-2 (created on demand)
3. **SHA-256 capture hashing**: Every capture includes a hash for diffing between runs without storing full transcripts
