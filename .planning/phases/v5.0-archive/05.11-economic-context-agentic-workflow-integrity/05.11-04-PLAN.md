  <name>Task 1: Implement comprehensive economic risk scoring with three components</name>
  <files>src/alphaswarm_sol/economics/risk.py, src/alphaswarm_sol/orchestration/output.py</files>
  <action>
Compute economic_risk (0-10) with three major components:

1. Base risk components (existing, enhanced):
   - value_at_risk (VAR): scaled by TVL or asset exposure (0-4)
   - privilege_concentration (PRIV): single-role admin vs multisig (0-2)
   - offchain_reliance (OFFCHAIN): oracle, relayer, committee (0-2)
   - governance_mutability (GOV): upgradeability, no timelock (0-1)
   - incentive_misalignment (INCENTIVE): profitable exploit vs cost (0-1)

2. Game-theoretic adjustment (NEW):
   - Use PayoffMatrix to compute attack expected value
   - If is_economically_rational (EV > 0): multiply base score by 1.5
   - If EV is highly positive (>10 ETH): multiply by 2.0
   - Add attack_ev_score component to breakdown

3. Causal amplification factor (NEW - linked to causal_analysis.py):
   - Multiply by loss_amplification_factor from causal chain
   - Factor based on AMPLIFIES edges (flash loans, leverage, etc.)
   - Cap at 3x to prevent score explosion

4. Store score + full breakdown in finding metadata and beads.
Ensure risk does NOT affect correctness or confidence, only prioritization.
  </action>
