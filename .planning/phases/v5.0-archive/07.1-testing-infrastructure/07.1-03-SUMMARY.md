## Decisions Made

1. **GA Gates Scope:** TestMetrics.meets_ga_gates() checks ONLY precision (>=85%), recall_critical (>=95%), recall_high (>=85%). Per context doc, recall_medium and recall_low are tracked targets, NOT gates for GA release.

2. **Schema Design:** Corpus schema v1.0 includes:
   - `contracts`: contract_id, source_path, category, vulnerability_class, added_date, corpus_version
   - `ground_truth`: gt_id, contract_id, function_id, vulnerability_pattern, severity, code_location, verified_by, verification_date
   - `test_runs`: run_id, run_date, corpus_version, model_used, precision, recall, recall_critical, f1_score, cost_usd, notes

3. **Benchmark Presets:** Three defaults matching context doc:
   - quick: 2 min, recent-audits only, 80% precision gate
   - standard: 10 min, all segments, 85% precision + 95% recall_critical gates
   - thorough: 30 min, all segments, multi-model (Opus + Sonnet), full GA gates
