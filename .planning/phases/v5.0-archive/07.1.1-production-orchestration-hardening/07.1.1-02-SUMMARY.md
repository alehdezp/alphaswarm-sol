## Decisions Made

- **Key format:** SHA256(pool_id + bead_id + action + payload_hash)[:16] for 16-char hex keys
- **Storage:** JSONL append-only for durability and audit trail (latest record per key = current state)
- **Stale reservations:** 10-minute timeout allows recovery from crashed processes
- **Permanent failures:** Marked as complete to prevent infinite retry loops
- **Jitter:** Random fraction (0-25% of delay) to prevent thundering herd on retries
