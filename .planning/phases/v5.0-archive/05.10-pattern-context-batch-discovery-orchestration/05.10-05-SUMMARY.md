## Decisions Made

1. **Evidence weight system**: Required ops have weight 1.0, strong ops 0.6, weak ops 0.3. This ensures required ops dominate the coverage score.

2. **Single-pass expansion policy**: Only one expansion attempt is allowed by default. This prevents runaway context growth while still allowing recovery from missing ops.

3. **Nodes with required ops always included**: The dilation algorithm includes nodes containing required operations regardless of their BFS distance. This ensures required evidence is never missed due to hop limits.

4. **Three ExpansionConfig presets**: default (0.8 threshold, radius 4, budget 100), conservative (0.9 threshold, radius 2, budget 50), aggressive (0.6 threshold, radius 6, budget 200).
