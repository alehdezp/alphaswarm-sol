## Tasks Completed

### Task 1: Define role contracts and diversity policy

- Extended `schemas/alphaswarm-codex-output.json` with role-specific definitions:
  - ScoutHypothesis: Fast pattern triage with confidence cap at 0.70
  - VerificationResult: Evidence-first validation with counter-signals
  - ContradictionReport: Refutation-only with typed counterarguments
  - CompositionProposal: Operation-signature algebra for composite vulnerabilities
  - SynthesizedFinding: Convergent evidence merging with confidence bounds

  - operation_first: Focus on semantic operations and behavioral signatures

  - invariant_first: Focus on protocol invariants and economic constraints
