## Decisions Made

- **Typed omissions include edge_type:** Per plan requirement, omission entries contain both edge_id and edge_type (e.g., "calls", "writes_state") to enable debugging why specific context was excluded
- **Witness ordering is deterministic:** All witness lists (node_ids, edge_ids, evidence_ids, operations) are sorted alphabetically to ensure reproducibility across builds
- **PatternSliceFocus.from_pcp() parses nested structures:** Handles both flat and nested PCP v2 data formats (op_signatures.required_ops, witness.minimal_required, etc.)
- **Completeness signal via is_complete:** Result tracks whether all required ops found, no forbidden ops detected, and no missing witness evidence
