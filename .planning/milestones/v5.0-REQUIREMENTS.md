## What This Means

v5.0 delivered **architecturally sound infrastructure** but closed without proving the system works end-to-end on real contracts. This honest closure enables:

1. **v6.0 "From Theory to Reality"** — Prove every capability works
2. **Evidence-first approach** — Replace claims with demonstration
3. **Focused scope** — Validate 10-20 patterns thoroughly vs. 556 untested
4. **Real contract testing** — DVDeFi suite, not mocks

The foundation is strong. Now we must prove it works.
