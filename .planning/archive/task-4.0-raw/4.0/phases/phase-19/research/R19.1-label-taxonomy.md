## Research Questions

1. Which labels produce the highest signal for complex logic risks?
2. Which labels are too broad and should be removed?
3. What minimum set enables policy mismatch detection?

---

## Proposed Label Classes

- INTENT labels (function purpose)
- CAPABILITY labels (what actions are possible)
- AUTHORITY labels (who can invoke)
- TRUST labels (external interaction level)
- STATE labels (state machine context)
- INVARIANT labels (expected correctness conditions)
- SEQUENCE labels (behavioral orderings)
