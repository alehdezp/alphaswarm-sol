## Objective

Define how to extract minimal business logic specifications from
code comments, docs, and configuration to produce intent and
invariant labels.

---

---

## Research Questions

1. Which doc locations provide reliable business constraints?
2. How to represent specs as minimal labels (no prose)?
3. How to tie specs to specific functions or variables?

---

## Proposed Heuristics

- Parse NatSpec tags (`@notice`, `@dev`) for constraints
- Extract config constants as `CONTEXT:LIMIT:*`
- Map role definitions to `AUTHORITY` labels
- Link event names to state transitions
